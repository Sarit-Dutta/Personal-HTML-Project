Music

Music is essentially my primary hobby outside of gaming as it has been an integral part of my life since childhood. Starting from age 6, my parents had me enrolled in a musical observatory where I learnt to play the piano and climbed up the levels within the Royal Conversatory of Music until I reached Level 8 where I decided to stop classes in favour of studying. However, even through the reduced practice times for piano, I found other ways to integrate music in my life, such as Youtube, or Spotify for example. Channels such as Kyle Landry, and Animenz who incorporated their skill in the musical arts and tied them to musical themes and motifs from films, television shows and video games which I had seen or played whilst growing up. It also exposed me to the rapper known as Watsky, whose first song I had heard as part of a web animated series made by Rooster Teeth. His artistic talents and visually imaginative lyrics brought a whole other sense of ease and sometimes melancholy to the world around me which kept me grounded, and I still listen to his music today, having only recently realizing the evolution he went through over the past 3 to 4 years, from his album Cardboard Castles, up until his most recent album Placement.
